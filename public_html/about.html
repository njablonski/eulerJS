About eulerJS

eulerJS is one man's mission to teach himself JavaScript with Project Euler. The challenges completed will impart knowledge of algorithms, mathematics, and the capabilities of JavaScript. Building this site to display solutions will teach more practical web development skills.

The front page will always show the six most recently completed algorithm challenges. You can find a full list of all completed challenges on the archives page. You can also follow the project and report issues on Github here.

eulerJS is a project of Nicholas Jablonski, a technical writer living in Seattle. Nicholas has worked on software documentation at Google, HBO, and Palantir Technologies. He has an eager and driving interest in algorithms and computing, and is open to hearing from you about your company or project.
